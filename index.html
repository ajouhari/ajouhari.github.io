Algorithms

Algorithms are much like the gears that keep a machine running. They are critical to many operations, and how they’re coded may save time when you can least afford it.

Many of our everyday tasks are rather menial, and can easily be automated or assisted by computer algorithms. Available is a list of algorithms that serve many different purposes. Each are labelled by their complexity, instructions, and the date at which they were created.

Game development

There’s is no better feeling than that of a breezy summer night as a kid, staying indoors and playing your favorite game. Games are a great way to relax and enjoy the moment, and that’s what I aim for when creating my video games.

- Stumble

Stumble is my first game development project, making this game took a great deal of trial and error. But eventually, I got a working model as a foundation to what will later become a fully fledged, multi-level game. This was produced using the Unity framework, and the Bolt and Sunnyland assets. The game is currently not supported on mobile and tablets.
